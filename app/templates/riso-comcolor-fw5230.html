RISO ComColor FW5230

The RISO ComColor FW5230 is part of the latest ComColor range of printers. ComColor FW5230 boasts ultra-fast print speed combined with a fast first print time to streamline printing, helping make busy work environments more efficient.

With print speeds at 120ppm for both simplex and duplex printing, duplex scanning and rich feature set, the RISO ComColor FW5230 brings productivity to any workplace. Additionally, the FW5230 has a far smaller footprint than it’s ComColor 7150 pre-decessor, enabling it to fit tin any workspace.

Its ultimately low power consumption prevents paper from curling to reduce paper jams. And because output requires no cooling, pages can be sent immediately after printing to any of your optional finishers, vastly reducing production times.

Features and Benefits of the RISO ComColor FW5230

• A3 multi-functional printer
• Full colour printing
• 120 pages per minute
• Up to 600 x 600 dpi resolution
• Simple, intuitive user interface
• First time print of only 5 seconds
• One-pass duplex scanner for faster scanning
• 200 sheet document feed
• Blank page detection, omitting blank pages from scans
• Print directly from USB
• Up to 1,000 user registration
• Energy Star Award assures energy efficiency
