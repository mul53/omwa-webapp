RISO ComColor GD9630

Forming part of the new RISO ComColor range, the ComColor GD9630 is the flagship device, printing up to 160 pages per minute. This print speed, marks it as the world’s fastest cut-sheet printer, producing 800 full colour single-sided pages in just 5 minutes!

The ComColor GD9630 uses a five-colour CYMK+grey system which allows for better image quality, with optimised blacks and halftones. Regardless of the number and types of images or volume of text, full-color images remain true to the original with the GD 9630.

User interactivity is both improved and simplified across the entire ComColor range with an easy to use colour LCD touch screen. Users can pre-set favourite settings that can be accessed from any mode, and multiple menus provide quick access to settings and pre-saved documents.

RISO ComColor GD9630 reduces power consumption to an all-time low with the introduction of an automatic power shut-off, using even less power than previous models, such as the ComColor 9150 and ComColor 7150. All ComColor machines are ENERGY STAR® certified, and boast many other energy-efficient innovations.

Features and Benefits of the RISO ComColor GD9630

• Energy efficient print solution
• 2,500 sheet standard capacity, for long uninterrupted runs
